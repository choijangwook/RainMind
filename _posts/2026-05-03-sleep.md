## 🌧 Rain Sounds for Deep Sleep

Struggling to fall asleep? Rain sounds are one of the most effective natural sleep aids.

The steady rhythm of rainfall helps calm your brain and reduce mental noise.

### Why Rain Helps Sleep

- Blocks background noise  
- Creates a consistent calming pattern  
- Reduces stress and anxiety  

### Best Way to Use

Use moderate volume and combine rain with low ambient sound for best results.
